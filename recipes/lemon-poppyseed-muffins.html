Lemon Poppyseed Muffins

Description

Soft, fluffy, vegan lemon poppyseed muffins.

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients

  • 3 cups (375g) all-purpose plain flour
  • 1 ¼ cups (250g) granulated sugar
  • 2 ½ tablespoons poppy seeds
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 2 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt

Wet Ingredients

  • 1 cup (250g) dairy-free mil
  • ⅔ cup (165g) neutral flavored oil
  • ¼ cup (60g) lemon juic
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Topping (Optional)

  • 3 tablespoons flaked almonds
  • 1 cup (100g) powdered sugar / icing sugar
  • 1-2 tablespoons lemon juice

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F, note 4). Arrange muffins liners in a muffin pan.
  2. Add all the dry ingredients to a large mixing bowl. Mix until well combined and the ingredients are evenly distributed.
  3. Add all of the wet ingredients to the bowl and mix until just combined.
  4. Use an ice cream scoop or a large spoon to divide the mixture into your muffin tin.
  5. Optional: sprinkle flaked almonds on top of your muffins.
  6. Place the muffin pan in the oven and immediately reduce the oven temperature to 180°C (350°F).
  7. Bake the muffins for around 22-25 minutes. They are ready when you can insert a toothpick into a muffin, and it comes out clean. Some moist crumbs on your toothpick are fine, but there shouldn't be any wet batter on it.
  8. Cool the muffins in the pan for 10 minutes then allow them to cool on a wire rack.
  9. Add the powdered sugar and lemon juice to a medium bowl. Whisk until combined. The icing should be runny but hold its shape for a few seconds when you drizzle it. To make the icing thicker, add 1 tablespoon of powdered sugar and mix again. To make the icing thinner, add a few drops of lemon juice and mix again.
  10. When the muffins have cooled, drizzle the icing on top.
  11. Enjoy the muffins warm or at room temperature. Note that the icing will melt if you warm up the muffins.
  12. Store the muffins in an airtight container at room temperature for 2 days, in the fridge for 3 days or in the freezer for up to 1 month.
